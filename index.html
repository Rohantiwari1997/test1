#1 Cloud Strategy
At ACC, We are helping leaders plan for continued volatility in the near and medium term and imagine what the postpandemic future could look like. We assist the transition to a cloud-based system by thoroughly analysing and comparing current IT portfolio and infrastructure with future state from a financial standpoint. We incorporate factors ranging from people, process and technology to provide an accurate picture of the financial benefits of cloud adoption.

#2 Cloud Security
Security is a primary concern for enterprises that want to adopt public cloud services. With our deep understanding and expertise in strategies for cloud adoption and security, we offer highly secure cloud solutions that address all your governance and technology needs.
#3 Application Migration
We help you to migrate your applications and workloads to the cloud in a quick, seamless and cost-effective manner. We provide storage services with the added cloud benefits of flexibility, performance, security and scalability.
#4 DevOps
Our DevOps tools and practices help you deliver applications and services at a much faster pace than traditional software development and infrastructure management processes. We also help you to easily set up automated pipelines to build, test and deploy your applications to any platform.
#5 Application Modernisation
We help you modernise your legacy applications for the cloud which leads to improved team productivity, reduced technical limitations and better customer experiences. Modernisation can also pave ways to new revenue sources.
